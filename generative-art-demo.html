Generative Art: Two Approaches

Same idea — visuals produced by code, not drawn by hand — rendered two different ways. Left: p5.js, a particle system evaluated on the CPU. Right: a GLSL fragment shader, evaluated per-pixel on the GPU, in the style of a Shadertoy sketch.

p5.js · CPU · flow-field particles

Particle Flow Field

Thousands of particles drift along a Perlin-noise vector field, each step drawn as a translucent line. Classic Processing/p5.js generative-art technique.

GLSL · GPU · fragment shader

Shadertoy-style Shader

Every pixel's color is computed independently, in parallel, from its screen position and elapsed time — no shapes or particles, just a per-pixel math function.
